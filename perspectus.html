Perspectus

An initiative of Thought Processors
Overview

It is rightly said, "It's all about your perspective." After all, viewing things in a different light can have a huge impact on your understanding of them. Take quantum mechanics, for instance, where the mere act of observation can completely change the outcome.

With that, we present to you Perspectus, the much-awaited second edition of Perplexor. Perspectus is an online cryptic hunt that comprises of 15 tantalizing puzzles. Participants will be required to make use of the clues scattered on the page to solve these puzzles as quickly as possible. Additional hints may be found on our Twitter page.

Please understand that we will be releasing additional hints only when we find that a large number of people are stuck at a certain level. The higher levels of the game have been designed in such a manner that finding the solution may require some time. Therefore, we ask all participants to be a little patient with the hints.

Remember to think outside the box, because there's almost always more to it than meets the eye!

Happy hunting!

Guidelines

Google is your best friend (as always). You can also reverse-search images.

Basic URL manipulation (.html, .jpg, etc.) may be required in some of the higher levels.

The clues for each level can usually be found in the:

  • Page Title
  • Page Source (Right-click on the page and select 'View Page Source')
  • Image Names
  • URL

Answers are case and space insensitive. That is, 'I love TP' and 'ilovetp' are interpreted in the same way. Answers may include special characters.

There are no restrictions on the number of answer attempts. So please, do not hesitate in trying out different answers. You never know what might work.

Sharing of answers is strictly prohibited. Anyone caught cheating will be disqualified from the event.
